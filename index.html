// ABOUT JAG

Defending the
Digital Frontier

JAG Security was founded by three computer science students passionate about cybersecurity. Our mission is to make advanced security tools accessible, educational, and practical for students and professionals alike.

From real-time threat monitoring to hands-on cryptography tools, JAG bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world security practice.

// OUR MISSION

Security for Everyone

We believe cybersecurity knowledge should be open, accessible, and practical. JAG provides the tools and education to understand threats and build defenses.
